CREATE ACCOUNT

      If you create an Emmergency Fund account, your accout details will be updated above with a "Pending" status until verifed by admin, only then, your account status will change to "Active", else your account details above will be blank.

After creating an Emmergency Fund account on your dashboard, create a "Deposit" ticket under the "Tickets page" to request approval by Admin, and the Admin will approve of your deposit process.

To know if your Emmergency Fund account is running, the account status will display "Active", if not, create an "Inquiry ticket" to know why from Admin.

Incase you would like to deposit or withdraw from your Emmergency Fund account, create a "Deposit" or "Withdrawal" ticket respectively and clearly indicate how much you would like to deposit or withdraw from your Emmergency Fund account.

The Emmergency Fund account grows at an interest of "5% per month and a 60% yearly interest, fluctuating and not guaranted" and is fully accessible for withdrawal at any time of any day considering there is no active performance, as communicated by Admin, else you can access fully, upto 50% of your Emmergency Funds.

For any inquiries or complaints, let the admin know by creating a corresponding ticket.
